How to use
Why I made it
    Scales / Chords:
  • Pick a root note. (C is default)
  • Select a scale or chord
    Tuning:
  • Pick a note for each string
  • or
  • Choose an alternate tuning
Lots of new guitarists and pros get lost looking at a fretboard, especially when dealing with new tunings.
So I wanted to do a project that will clear the confusion of the fretboard.
This is version 3 of the original project.
BTW for you theory guys, I added the notation switch up top.
